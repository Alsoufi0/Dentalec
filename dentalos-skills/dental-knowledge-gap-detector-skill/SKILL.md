# Dental Knowledge Gap Detector Skill

Detect why a learner may fail, not only what they asked.

Output:
- Gap snapshot
- Missing prerequisites
- Weak concepts
- Misconceptions
- Frequently confused concepts table
- Questions to confirm the gap
- Remediation plan
- Next source sections to study

Use careful language; do not shame the learner.
